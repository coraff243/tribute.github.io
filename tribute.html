Tribute to John Ndung'u

John Ndungu was an incredible individual who made a significant impact on my life. He was a caring and thoughtful individual who you never once thought fought among the Mau Mau rebellion due to how he had a peaceful demeanor and was very humble. Though he bore some scars from the war, both physically and emotionally, I admire how he brought up my father and his brothers to be strong men willing to whether any challenges. I will never forget the days my relatives and I would stay till morning just to listen to his war stories. It was also great to see that till the end even when suffering from lung cancer he went through it all courageously . We will never forget you ; you shall stick in our hearts forever. Rest in peace.
